About Me

My Full-Stack Developer Journey

After 20 years in my previous career I decided I needed a change,and I found coding, I am currently self-learning how to develope & design web-sites, this self-learning is done through on-line tutorials and Youtube videos, in September 2022 i will be taking this development further by starting a Professional Diploma course in Full Stack Software Developement with University College Dublin/Code Institute.

After a Baker apprenticeship, I moved into healthcare, firstly as a Security officer for 15 years and then 6 years in a clerical role, then 2 years as a stay-at-home parent, I decided I needed a change and a big one.

When I found out about the Full Stack Developer course I watched a few on-line tutorials which i found to be very interesting so I completed the 5 Day Challenge with the Code Institute and passed without much difficulty.

So I decided, sign me up, lets do this and I havent stoped coding since...
